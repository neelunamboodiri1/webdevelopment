Our Founder

Zoobox was founded in 2018 by a Bay Area High School math, engineering, and compuer science teacher named Joey Knapp (pictured right). Being a man of many talents and hobbies, Mr. Knapp enjoyed eating and cooking extic meats from around the world. Understanding how difficult it is to aquire these exotic meats Mr. Knapp came up for the idea of Zoo Box as a way to make the exotic meat buying process easier.



How does Zoo Box work?

Zoo Box is a sevice that sends exotic meats straight to your doorstep. Once you have made a Zoobox account, you are ready to order. First you pick your monthly plan (see pricing section). Then you look through our meat menu to see which exotic meats you want in your box for the month. The meat catalog is uodated each month with new meats choices that re based on popularity and what's in season. It's simple and very affordable.





Our Mission


Our mission is to bring the finest and freshest cuts of exotic meat's from all over the world right to your doorstep. Speed and Satisfaction is everything to our company.
